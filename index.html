Building an Inclusive, Productive and Compassionate Society where Widows and Orphans are Empowered

To empower widows and orphans through education, agriculture, food production and packaging, skills acquisition, job creation, and shelter, restoring dignity, transforming mindsets, and providing sustainable pathways back to purposeful living. With these, we are building a Society where everybody thrives.
